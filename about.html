About Us

“Care that feels like home.”

Call us Enquire Online

We’re dedicated to being the trusted choice for compassionate home care, helping you or your loved one live independently, safely, and with dignity.

At 1st Choice Residential Services, our mission is to be The most trusted provider of compassionate, person-centred home care — empowering individuals to live independently, safely, and with dignity in the comfort of their own homes. We believe that true care goes beyond daily support; it’s about building meaningful connections, fostering trust, and respecting each person’s unique story. Every day, we strive to deliver care that feels personal, dependable, and full of heart — ensuring every client feels valued, respected, and truly at home.

Our Services

Personalized Mental Health Support

We provide tailored emotional and behavioral care to help ease anxiety, build confidence, and promote overall mental well-being. Each client receives support that is unique to their needs and circumstances.

Companionship & Social Engagement

Our caregivers focus on fostering meaningful connections through conversations, hobbies, and community activities. Consistent companionship helps reduce loneliness and enriches emotional health.

Daily Living Assistance

From meal preparation and bathing to medication reminders and mobility support, our trained caregivers help clients maintain independence while ensuring their daily needs are met with dignity and care.

Medical Appointment & Medication Support

We assist with scheduling and attending medical appointments, as well as managing medications, so clients can stay healthy, on track, and stress-free.

Skill-Building & Developmental Activities

Our team engages clients in activities designed to stimulate creativity, nurture new skills, and encourage personal growth, boosting confidence and overall quality of life.
